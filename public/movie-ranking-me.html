Farid Beck's Best Movies

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

A Bridge Too Far

This is my all-time memorable movie because it is the first movie I watched when I was still a kid in Primary 3. My uncle was the one who brought me to watch this movie.

Qurbani

This was the first ever Hindi movie I watch which started my journey in Hindi films and songs. My friends in Junior college who were mostly Indians recommended me and from then on I was addicted to Hindi films and songs.

Killing Fields

This was the first movie I watched after I received the news that I am not required to perform National Service.

As all my college friends had been already enlisted to NS, I was feeling rather alone and dejected. So I decided to cheer myself up and I went to watch this movie all alone.
